## Getting Started with Bazai

Bazai is a comprehensive platform that empowers developers and teams to build, test, and manage intelligent AI agents and generative tools. It provides an intuitive playground environment where you can experiment with AI capabilities, integrate various APIs, and deploy your creations seamlessly. Whether you're a beginner exploring AI development or an experienced engineer refining complex systems, Bazai offers the tools you need to bring your AI ideas to life.
